The Way of the Mysteries

CORRESPONDENCE COURSE APPLICATION

Application is by email, payment for each pair of lessons (£ 40 sterling for first two lessons) is  made by credit card

Here's the easy way to do it!

Send us your order for the first two lessons , plus your shipping details and, if you want to, tell us something about yourself. ..we like to get to know the folk that we will be working with.  So go to the  the application form (click here) , fill it out and send to: harrisavalon@btinternet.com

The Application form has a built in credit card link if you want to apply and purchase the first two lessons now

 

We can only  accept  credit card payments. As soon as your application and payment are accepted, we'll send a confirmation to you ,  and advise on the dispatch of  your lesson material. 

 

.

Orders for our lesson CD's are accepted on the following terms and conditions:

PLEASE NOTE THAT THESE LESSON CD's ARE DATA CD'S IN PDF FORMAT. THEY CAN BE RUN ON ANY PC, WITH A PDF READER, SUCH AS ACROBAT. ALL CD'S ARE TESTED BEFORE THEY LEAVE US.  

 We regret that once the CD  has been dispatched we cannot agree to any refunds. However, if your lessons are  not received within 21 days, or are damaged or you are unable to run the CD, email us at harrisavalon@btinternet.com and we shall answer any queries and/or arrange to re-supply the lesson material to you.  We reserve the right, without explanation,  to refuse (and to refund) and/or terminate instruction  to any student or intending student whom we consider to be unsuitable for this course.  You must be at least eighteen years old to apply and, as with most lodges in the Western Tradition, we prefer our students be at least twenty five years old and have a measure of "real life experience" to be able to fully benefit from our instruction.
