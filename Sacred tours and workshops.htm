Sacred Tours and Workshops

The Principals of The Company of Avalon...Mike Harris and Steve Blamires  are established authors and lecturers in Celtic mythology and the magic of the Western Tradition . Between them they have an enormous wealth of experience in hosting workshops and guiding sacred tours. For those who wish to get a first hand , introductory view of the Mysteries associated with Avalon and/or to tread the landscapes in which these mysteries came into being, Mike and Steve each, independently, offer the facilities to do so.

Mike Harris       

     

Mike Harris has for many years conducted  workshops on the Mysteries in the UK and USA and acts as a specialist guide to a number of sacred tours. Guest speaking, workshops and guide facilities may be available by arrangement.  Living in the breathtaking fastness of Snowdonia in North Wales, he also  provides tailored excursions covering the sacred sites in Snowdonia and Anglesey from which the Mabinogion, Taliesin and Arthurian /Merlin myths originated. These  specialist "Magic on the Land" excursions are available for small private groups and individuals by arrangement. For further details

go to http://mikeharris.companyofavalon.net

or contact Mike at harrisavalon@btinternet.com 

Steve Blamires           Steve Blamires gives regular talks, courses and discussions on Celtic mythology and spirituality, along with courses on the Arthuriad, Grail Mysteries and the Western Mysteries, throughout the Pacific Northwest, California and western Canada. He does many 'formal' classes with local colleges and schools but also offers his own in-depth courses of a more 'hands-on' nature for students who want more than just the theory and references. During the summer months he leads groups to some of the more remote and mystical parts of the UK, Ireland, Iceland and Greenland. Steve can be contacted through his business, "Wandering Angus" at www.wanderingangus.com or through the Company of Avalon's website
