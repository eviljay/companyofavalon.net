Dion Fortune, the Avalonians and Faerie

September 1st 2007 9.30am.

The Assembly Rooms, High Street, Glastonbury.

 

 In Avalon of the Heart Dion Fortune speaks of being much moved by attending a performance of William Sharp's Faerie epic The Immortal Hour, set to music by Rutland Boughton and performed at the Assembly Rooms at Glastonbury, this year's seminar venue.  Our theme this year therefore explores the understanding of the world of Faerie held by Dion Fortune and her fellow Avalonians ..and successors.

 

Speakers will include:

Gareth Knight

Alan Richardson

Steve Blamires

Mike Harris
